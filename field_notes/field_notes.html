Your guide to the best picnic fields in Bath










The Selection






BATHWICK MEADOW

A fantastic field owned by the National Trust. Great sunset views. You can walk down from campus through this field.

Softness of grass: 3/5

Quietness: 3/5

Nature sounds: 4/5

FIELD NEAR SHAM CASTLE

This field is near Sham Castle, next to the golf course. It is an ideal spot to bask in the sunlight for an hour or two in between lectures.

Softness of grass: 5/5

Quietness: 5/5

Nature sounds: 4/5

CRESCENT LAWN

A fantastic field in front of the Crescent. We have watched a hot air balloon being filled up here, too.

Softness of grass: 5/5

Quietness: 2/5

Nature sounds: 3/5
