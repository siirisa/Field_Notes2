Hello, stranger!


If you have landed on our page it means you must be a fellow enthusiast for picnics. Or perhaps you are just someone looking to escape the chaos of city life for a few hours.

We understand you perfectly, and that is how this website was born. Sometimes there is no better way to recharge than lay in the field, open a bottle of wine and soak up the sunrays while enjoying good company and food. This is the foolproof method to enjoy life that Emma, Siiri and Carolina experienced during their first year of university. We want to share with whomever needs a timeout from the crowds where to find the best spots in Bath to have a picnic.

More about us? The three of us met in the first year of University in lectures. We are management students from Northern Ireland, Finland, and Italy.
